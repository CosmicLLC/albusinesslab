# AI Biz Lab -- Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Overview

AI Biz Lab is an AI consulting and education platform designed to help
medium to large businesses adopt and implement artificial intelligence.
The platform combines a marketing website, an AI School (programmatic
SEO engine), and an admin panel for content management.

## Objectives

-   Generate enterprise leads through discovery calls
-   Establish authority in AI consulting
-   Build organic traffic via AI School content
-   Provide structured AI transformation services
-   Enable scalable content publishing via Supabase

## Target Users

-   CEOs, COOs, VPs, Directors
-   Operations, Marketing, and Sales leaders
-   Medium to large organizations exploring AI adoption

## Core Features

### 1. Marketing Website

-   Homepage
-   Services page
-   AI Transformation System page
-   Training page
-   About page
-   Contact page

### 2. AI School (Programmatic SEO Engine)

-   Dynamic article pages
-   Category pages
-   Internal linking structure
-   SEO-optimized routing
-   Supabase-backed content

### 3. Admin Panel (CMS-lite)

-   Create/edit articles
-   Manage categories
-   Publish/unpublish content
-   Slug management
-   Supabase integration

### 4. Lead Generation System

-   Contact form
-   Booking integration (Google Calendar / Calendly)
-   CTA placement across pages

## Tech Stack

-   Frontend: Next.js (App Router)
-   Styling: Tailwind CSS
-   Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
-   Auth: Supabase Auth
-   Deployment: Replit

## AI School (SEO Strategy)

-   Target business-intent AI keywords
-   Publish daily articles
-   Use topic clusters:
    -   AI strategy
    -   AI automation
    -   AI adoption
    -   AI use cases by department
-   Implement internal linking between articles and services pages

## Admin Panel Requirements

-   Authentication via Supabase
-   CRUD operations for articles and categories
-   Publish/unpublish toggle
-   Simple UI for content editing

## Conversion Strategy

-   Primary CTA: Book a Discovery Call
-   Secondary CTA: Contact form
-   CTAs placed on:

    -   Services pages
    -   AI School articles
    -   Footer

-   Embed Google Calendar or Calendly
-   Accessible across the site

-   Clean typography and spacing
